CHICKEN KARAHI

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken 1 Kg
  • Tomatoes 4
  • Yogurt 1/2 Cup
  • Garlic Ginger Paste 1 tbsp
  • Black Pepper 1+1/2 tsp
  • Crushed Button Red Chili 1+1/2 tsp
  • Salt as to taste
  • Garam Masala Powder 1/2 tsp
  • Lemon Juice 1 tbsp
  • Chopped Ginger as You Required
  • Green Coriander 2 tbsp (Chopped)
  • Green Chilli 4 (Chopped)
  • Fried Onion 2 tbsp
  • Oil 1/4 Cup

INSTRUCTION/METHOD

  1. Heat oil in a wok and add the garlic and ginger paste, stir fry for few seconds.
  2. Then add crushed coriander and cumin, stir well.
  3. Now add chicken, cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add green chilies (with one slit down) and fresh coriander and mix it well.
  5. Add roughly grind tomatoes, salt, crushed red chili and turmeric.
  6. Cook covered with lid till the chicken is done, and the water has dried
  7. Garnish with sliced ginger,green chilies and fresh coriander.
  8. Serve hot with chappati or naan.

CAJUN CHICKEN PASTA

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups cooked pasta (I like penne)
  • 1 1/2 lbs cubed chicken
  • 1 each - red bell pepper, green bell pepper, onion
  • 2 - 3 Tbsp Cajun Seasoning
  • 2 cups cream
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup flour
  • Salt to taste

INSTRUCTION/METHOD

  1. Cook pasta; drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté vegetables in hot olive oil. Season with 1/2 - 1 Tbsp Cajun seasoning while vegetables are cooking.
  3. Cook until soft.
  4. Season chicken with cajun seasoning.
  5. Add to soften vegetables. Add additional olive oil if needed.
  6. Cook until chicken is 80 - 90% done.
  7. Slowly add cream to chicken and vegetables.
  8. Bring to a rapid boil, then begin sprinkling in flour until desired thickness is achieved.
  9. Serve over cooked pasta

CHICKEN CORN SOUP

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 250 gram chicken with bones
  • 5-6 cups of water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder, leveled
  • 1.2 tsp Chinese salt (Ajinomoto), optional
  • 2 egg whites
  • 4-5 tbsp. corn flour or as required for thickness
  • 1/3 cup sweet corn, crushed
  • 3-4 tbsp. white vinegar with 2-3 green chilies
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • 3-4 tbsp. white vinegar with 1 tbsp. red chili paste/sauce
  • Papdi (optional)

INSTRUCTION/METHOD

  1. In a pot, add 5-6 cups of water, salt, ginger garlic paste and chicken. Let it boil. Then cook this mixture on medium flame for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Remove chicken pieces from stock, and shred into small pieces. Set this aside.
  3. Into 4-5 tbsp. of Corn flour (add more if required) add water and make a smooth mixture of it. Make sure there are no lumps.
  4. In a pestle or mortar, crush sweet corn and set this aside. (Add whole corn if you prefer).
  5. Now into chicken stock (yakhni) add 1 tsp salt, black pepper powder, and Chinese salt, shredded chicken and crushed corn. Mix it well and let it boil.
  6. Start adding corn flour mixture slowly and stir continuously to prevent lumps. Adjust consistency according to your preference.
  7. In the end, add egg whites and stir continuously so that egg whites will distribute evenly.
  8. Cook on medium flame for another 2 minute.
  9. Serve hot with, vinegar, chili sauce, soy sauce and Papdi.

WONTON

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken boneless 250 gms
  • Hara pyaz (Green onion) chopped 2 Cups
  • Hari mirch (Green chilies) 7-8
  • Shimla mirch (Capsicum) 1 medium
  • Soy sauce 1 tbs
  • Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) ½ tsp
  • Chicken cube ½
  • Oil 1 tsp
  • Namak (Salt) ¼ tsp or to taste
  • Anda (Egg) beaten 1
  • Wonton wraps
  • Oil for frying

INSTRUCTION/METHOD

  1. Pat dry chicken boneless cubes with kitchen tissue.
  2. In chopper,add chicken boneless cubes,green onion,green chilies,capsicum,soy sauce,black pepper powder,chicken cube,oil,salt and chop until well combined & set aside.
  3. Take wanton wrap,add 1 tbs mixture in the center,apply egg at the edges,fold in triangular shape & seal all sides.
  4. Apply egg on one side of triangle and overlap to form wonton shape (makes 25-30).
  5. In wok,heat oil and fry until golden brown.
  6. Can be stored up to one month in freezer.

CHICKEN NUGGETS

INGREDIENTS

  • Bread Slices 3
  • Milk ½ Cup
  • Chicken Cubes 500 Gms
  • Salt 1 Tsp
  • Black Pepper ½ Tsp
  • Paprika ½ Tsp
  • Soy Sauce 1 Tsp
  • Garlic Powder ½ Tsp
  • Dried Parsley ½ Tsp
  • Egg 1
  • Bread Crumbs 1 Cup
  • Salt ¼ Tsp
  • Black Pepper 1 Pinch
  • Dried Parsley ½ Tsp
  • Flour ½ Cup
  • Chicken Powder ½ Tsp
  • Corn flour 1 Tbs
  • Egg 1
  • Oil For Frying

INSTRUCTION/METHOD

  1. In bowl, add shredded bread slices and milk, soak bread in milk and set aside
  2. In food chopper, add chicken and chop it for 1-2 mins
  3. Add soaked bread, chop little more
  4. Add garlic powder, salt, paprika, black pepper powder, soy sauce, dried parsley, egg and chop it for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Take out the mixture in a bowl and refrigerate for 30-45 minutes
  6. Take a bowl add flour, corn flour, chicken powder, mix and set aside
  7. In another bowl, take breadcrumbs ,add black pepper, salt, dried parsley, mix and set aside
  8. In a separate bowl, Take 1 egg and whisk it and set aside.
  9. Grease your hands with oil properly, take chicken mixture (as per desired size) and shape it.
  10. Coat in flour mixture, dip in beaten egg then coat in breadcrumbs.
  11. Prepare all nuggets and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  12. At this stage, you can freeze nuggets for up to 30 days. Whenever you need to fry them take them out, thaw for 10 mins
  13. Shallow or deep fry until nice golden color appear
